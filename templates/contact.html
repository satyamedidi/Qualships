What types of services does Qualships offer?

Qualships specializes in ship repair, marine surveying, marine engineering, major marine project management, and various marine services. Our comprehensive range of expertise covers everything from routine maintenance to complex repair projects.

What sets Qualships apart from other ship repair companies

Qualships distinguishes itself through its extensive experience, highly skilled team, and commitment to excellence. As Australia's premier ship repair and marine surveying company, we offer unmatched expertise, top-quality service, and innovative solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.

How does Qualships ensure the quality of its services?

Quality is a top priority at Qualships. We maintain rigorous quality control measures at every stage of our operations, from initial assessment to project completion. Our team of experienced professionals, including marine engineers, master mariners, ship fitters, welders, lifeboat technicians, and electricians, adhere to industry standards and best practices to deliver superior outcomes consistently.

Absolutely. With our extensive experience in major marine project management, Qualships is well-equipped to handle projects of any scale. Our team has the expertise, resources, and capabilities to manage complex projects efficiently, ensuring timely delivery and exceptional results.
